Delicious Dessert Recipes

Classic Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Melt butter in a microwave-safe bowl
  3. Mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla
  4. Add cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder
  5. Pour into greased 8-inch square pan
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes
  7. Let cool before cutting into squares

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 vanilla bean or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 large egg yolks

Steps:

  1. Combine cream, milk, sugar, and salt in a saucepan
  2. Heat until sugar dissolves, stirring occasionally
  3. Whisk egg yolks in a separate bowl
  4. Slowly add warm cream mixture to egg yolks
  5. Return mixture to saucepan and cook until thickened
  6. Strain and stir in vanilla
  7. Chill thoroughly
  8. Churn in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions
  9. Freeze until firm
